Classic (ICS / FB-A)

Vertical Tubeless Steam Boiler
The Classic is Fulton's original vertical tubeless boiler, available for steam or hot water applications. Simple, compact, and trouble-free, more than 100,000 Classic boilers have been sold around the world to virtually every type of industry imaginable. The Classic features efficiencies to 80% and oil and/or gas capabilities with Low Emissions burner (gas only), all packaged and trimmed for your heavy-duty application.
