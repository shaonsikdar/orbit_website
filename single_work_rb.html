RB

Horizontal fuel-fired reverse flame wet-back steam boiler
The RB series boiler is designed by the engineers in Fulton Great Britain. RB series boiler bears comparison with all other similar boilers, wherever in the world. The overall design criteria of high operating efficiency and a boiler that will last for many years means the RB has many unique features and performance characteristics. The specification is superior to and exceeds the minimum construction standards of BS 2790 or ASME.
RB series boiler is a wet-back reverse flame horizontal boiler. With large furnace volume, large furnace surface area, the furnace exit temperature is reduced via enhanced radiation and convection, and front gas chamber condition is much better than the regular reverse fire boilers.
It has large number of fire tubes and large tube total cross-sectional area. It’s water level probe directly immerses into pressure vessel, which avoids false water level, and increases operating security.
Name: Horizontal fuel-fired reverse flame wet-back steam boiler
Number: RB
Manufactured by: Fulton China
Steam output: 1t/h-5t/h (60BHP-300BHP)
Standard MWP: 1.0MPa, other pressure upon to customer’s request
